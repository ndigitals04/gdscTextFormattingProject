how to build an engaged audience on instagram

without talking on your stories

  • post a "hi it's me" on your feed to reintrodce yourself every so often
  • include personal anecdotes in your caption
  • add a "about me" highlight with photos of you or fast facts about your life and biz
  • post photos of your work or living space on stories
  • include polls, question boxes, quizzes on your story about all sorts of topics, both work and personal
  • post about your daily to-do's and tasks
